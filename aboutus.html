About Us.
UNDER THE SEA

Under the sea is the first organization that cruise services and water activities for tourists that focus on marine life especially orca whales and blue whales. Apart from providing tourism services such as vehicles and accommodation, we also spread knowledge about the population and habitat of orca and blue whales to our tourists. Various activities and services that we provide to ensure the comfort and enjoyment of tourists throughout the holiday.

Vision : To provide awareness to the community about the declining population of whales due to the self important nature of some communities apart from imparting new knowledge to them.
Mission : To ensure the community is aware of the importance of caring for marine life
